Project Steps

Discover how we developed our Drop It Forward project, from investigation to final reflection.

1

Phase 1 – Investigation

We started by identifying the problem of expensive water access in our community. We did research, conducted surveys, and analyzed residents' needs to better understand the situation.

2

Phase 2 – Planning

We then designed the rainwater collection system by preparing a plan, budget, materials list, and design sketches. We also received advice from our mentor to improve the project.

3

Phase 3 – Action

We built and installed the rainwater harvesting system using available materials, then explained its operation to the community and collected their feedback.

4

Phase 4 – Reflection

Finally, we evaluated our work by analyzing strengths and difficulties encountered. We also thought about how to improve the project for the future.
