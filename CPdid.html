What We Did

A summary of our actions and results for the Drop It Forward project.

🔍

Research and Survey

We analyzed the water access problem, conducted community surveys, and identified the main needs.

📝

Planning

We designed the system, established a budget, and selected the necessary materials to build our rainwater collection system.

🏗️

Construction and Installation

We set up the functional system and explained to the community how to use it for their daily needs.

🌱

Impact and Reflection

We evaluated the system's effectiveness, considered possible improvements, and measured the positive impact on the community and sustainability.
