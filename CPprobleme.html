The Problem

Many families in our community have limited access to water or have to pay significant amounts to get it. This makes daily tasks more difficult.

~~o~~o~~o~~

High Cost

For some families, buying drinking water represents a significant expense each month, limiting other essential needs.

Limited Access

In some areas, water is not always available, forcing residents to travel or ration their usage.

Health Issues

Insufficient or poor quality water leads to health risks and affects daily hygiene.
